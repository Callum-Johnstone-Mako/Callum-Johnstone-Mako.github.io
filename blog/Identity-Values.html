Core personal values

  • Explain a situation where you have had an ethical decision to make. Discuss how you weighed up the values involved in that decision, the decision you made, and the consequences of your action.
  • An ethical decision I had to make happend a couple of years ago when the lady sitting on the bus infront of me left were laptop behind on the bus.

    The choice I had to make was whether to:
    1. Do nothing and leave it on the bus.
    2. Hand the laptop into the bus driver and hope he can find the owner.
    3. Take the laptop with me and track down the owner myself.

    I chose option three and took the laptop with me and find the owner myself. I came to this decision because if the situation was reversed I would have wanted the same thing to happen to me. Treting people the way you want to be treated is a core value I hold. I ended up finding th eowner from the username on facebook and returned the laptop to a relieved first year student.

  • Describe how your culture has influenced your values and identity.
  • Coming from a western culture my identity has been influenced by the society I live in. The Value/Identity most influenced would be my individualistic approach to most things in my life. Asking for help is not something that comes easy to me and I like to solve any problems I'm having myself.

Strengths and Limitations

  • Evaluate your strengths and limitations in terms of your learning and career development
  • I think my greatest strength when learning is my problem solving skills, I enjoy a challenge and coming up with a solution is extremely satisfing. A weakness I have that can affect my learning and career is that I can struggle to ask for help. I enjoy learning in a very hands on approach as opposed to the traditional listening and taking notes format of schools and universities. This means I can struggle or thrive depending on the learning situation.

    • As explained above I think my problem solving skills will be critical throughout this course but can also lead to road blocks when I dont ask for help when I cant find the solution myself.
