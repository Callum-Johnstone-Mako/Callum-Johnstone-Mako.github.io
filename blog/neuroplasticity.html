Neuroplasticity

  • Benefits of Neuroplasticity - Neuroplasticity is beneficial to our cognition in every way. Some of these benefits include: Helping to learn new skills, Improving mental health, Helps us engage activities more thoughtfully and increaseing our brains memory.

  • How to use Neuroplasticity - Having a better understanding of Neuroplasticity can work to our benfits when learning new skills. Being able to learn new skills quickly can be an important part in most careers if one wants to be successful.

  • How to increase Neuroplasticity - To use increase our Neuroplasticity there are certain lifestyle choices that can remote the regeneration of nerve cells. These include things like regular exercise and a healthy diet. Another way to increase Neuroplasticity is to use "Brain Training Games" suchs as Sudoku to create targeted mental stimualtion and improve connections between neurons.

    • Growth Mindset

  • What is Growth Mindset? - A growth mindset means that you thrive from challenges and dont see failure as something to be halted by. Instead we can use our failures as tools for growing our skills and talents. Having a growth mindstate over a static one is important as having the ability to grow from failures and belief in ones ability to grow plays a big role in a positive mindstate and everyday life.

  • Researching Growth Mindest - Something surpriseing to me while researching this idea of Growth Mindstate is that I discovered fall into the category of a fixed mindstate. I remember many times in my life where I have failed or not performed as well as I should have and became discouraged and gave up easily. As I have matured I think that I have started moving towards a growth mindstate but still have lots of room to improve. The tips from this website www.wgu.com to develop a growth mindstate have been quite handy in improving my mindstate.

  • Integrating Growth Mindset - Moving foward I will be using different methods from the resource above to develop my growth mindstate and try to remove my fixed mindstate. Using different techniques while learning new skills such as: Listening to my inner critic more and realising when the fixed mindset is taking over.
