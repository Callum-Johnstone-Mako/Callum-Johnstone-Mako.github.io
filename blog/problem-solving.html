    • How is EQ different to IQ?

    The key difference btween EQ and IQ is that our Emotional Intelligence measures our ability to recognze our emotions and the emotions of others. It also measures our awareness to make good decisions based on those emotions. IQ however measures our ability to solve problems, use logic and our ability to understand complex ideas.

    Why is EQ important?

    Our EQ plays an important part in our lives and having a high level of emotional Intelligence will help improve them in various areas. EQ can imrpove our ability to build relationships and reduce the stress that can come from them. Being able to understand our co workers emotions and feelings can also improve our wrokplace and productivity as solving issues and defusing conflicts will become easier with a high EQ. Without the ability to understand and control our emotions with a high EQ we can become more susceptible to poor mental states and can result in depression and anxiety.
