To create a functional website you need three key elements:

  1. HTML: The structure or skeleton of the webpage.
  2. CSS: The stlying of the webpage.
  3. Jsvascript: The functionality of the webpage.
A common analogy to understand these three components is to think of them as a building instead:

  • HTML: The framing of the building including how many rooms, windows, doors etc.
  • CSS: How the building can be designed using different paints and carpets and such.
  • Javascript: What can be done in the building. We could cook a meal, watch tv or sleep in a bed.
